Annoucement: No School Tomorrow

The College Station campus will be closed tomorrow due to inclimate weather.

Why is School Closed

The National Weather Service (NWS) has issued a Winter Weather Warning for Brazos County until the afternoon on Wednesday. There is a danger of icy roads in the morning, which makes travelling on the roads dangerous

What is Closed?

Things that are closed tomorrow include:

  • TAMU Libraries
  • University Recreation Sports Activities
  • TAMU Bus Services

Official Announcement

Texas A&M University posted information about the closure of the university through the Code Marron system to notify students and employees.
